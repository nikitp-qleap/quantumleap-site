Questions

Frequently Asked

Will I get less than my home is worth?

Not necessarily — that's the point. Because we offer flexible terms instead of demanding a cash discount, our creative offers are frequently higher than a typical cash buyer would pay.

What does "creative financing" actually mean?

We're not limited to an all-cash purchase or a traditional bank sale. We can structure the purchase using seller financing, taking over existing payments, or other flexible terms — often a better overall outcome for you.

Is selling on terms safe?

Every transaction is handled with proper contracts, title companies, and full transparency. You'll always know exactly how the deal is structured before you agree to anything.

My house needs repairs. Is that a problem?

Not at all. We buy homes in any condition — you won't need to fix, clean, or stage anything.

Do you charge any fees or commissions?

No agent commissions and no fees to you. We make our return through the investment itself.
