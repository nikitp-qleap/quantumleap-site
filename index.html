Questions

Good Questions, Honest Answers

Will I get less than my home is worth?

Not necessarily — that's the whole point. Because we offer flexible terms instead of demanding a cash discount, our creative offers are often higher than a typical cash buyer pays.

Do I need to fix anything first?

Nope. We buy homes as-is, in any condition. No repairs, no cleaning, no staging.

Are there any fees or commissions?

None to you. No agent commissions, no surprise fees. We make our return through the investment itself.

Is selling on terms safe?

Yes. Everything is done with proper contracts, title companies, and full transparency — you'll know exactly how it works before you agree to anything.

What if I'm not sure yet?

That's perfectly fine. Reach out, get your options, and take your time. There's never any obligation.
